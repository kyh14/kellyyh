I'm Kelly. I'm a techie living in SF. I have been an avid reader of long form essays. In the last couple of years, I've been avidly writing as well. I started with scribbles in my journals, then essays at workshops in the Writing Pad, and nowadays papers for business school. While each form of writing was so different in its context and intent, the process for all has affected my life in such a positive way. Here, I hope to keep up the writing bug and share my reflections.
